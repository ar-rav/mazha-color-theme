Recommended Fonts

The recommended fonts for Mazha the Plant Boutique are:

  • Manjari - A Malayalam and Latin family font whose name means pearl in Malayalam. It's also the name of a poetic metre. Recommended for headings due to its elegant and distinctive style, and cultural relevance.

    • The Quick Brown Fox Jumps Over The Lazy Dog. മിഴിയിലൊരു മുത്ത് തിളങ്ങും പോലെ.

  • Verdana / Tahoma - A clean, highly legible sans-serif font designed for screen readability. Suggested for body text to ensure clarity and ease of reading across various devices. Use caution when used without microsoft services due to copyright considerations.

    • The Quick Brown Fox Jumps Over The Lazy Dog. മിഴിയിലൊരു മുത്ത് തിളങ്ങും പോലെ. (Verdana)

    The Quick Brown Fox Jumps Over The Lazy Dog. മിഴിയിലൊരു മുത്ത് തിളങ്ങും പോലെ. (Tahoma)

  • Amarna - A semi-glyphic humanist sans-serif typeface that combines the sophistication of early humanist typefaces with modern design principles. Suitable for both headings and body text, especially in larger sizes.

    • The Quick Brown Fox Jumps Over The Lazy Dog.

  • Montserrat - A typeface inspired by the urban typography of the Montserrat neighborhood in Buenos Aires. It captures the essence of early twentieth-century natural design and is available under a libre license. Ideal for headlines and display text.

    • The Quick Brown Fox Jumps Over The Lazy Dog.

These fonts can be sourced from Google Fonts for easy web integration.
